  • High integration

    The A28 integrates dual-core CPU, 2x2 MIMO 2.4GHz & 5GHz Wi-Fi baseband and MAC, dual-band RF, Balun, built-in power amplifier, low-noise amplifier, filter, transceiver switch, and provides the ultimate single-frequency router for dual-band routers. Chip solutions.

    A28 uses dual-core quad processors, the highest frequency is 1GHz, with high-speed WiFi module and hardware forwarding engine, provides stable 866Mbps+300Mbps wireless access and full gigabit routing forwarding performance, supports up to 128 concurrent connections for users, operating temperature range -40℃ To +125℃.
